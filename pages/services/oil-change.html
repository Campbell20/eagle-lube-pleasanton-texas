Oil Changes at Eagle Lube

At Eagle Lube, we provide more than just an oil change. Our premium service oil change is a comprehensive preventive maintenance insepction that helps maintain the performance and health of your car. In order to properly maintain your vehicle, Eagle Lube suggests an oil change and preventive maintenance insepction every 3,000 miles, or 3 months, whichever comes first. We use a synthetic blend motor oil at Eagle Lube. We also carry a complete line of Penzoil synthetic motor oil. We understand your schedule may not permit you to spend an afternoon waiting for your vehicle to be serviced. At Eagle Lube, we strive to complete each premium service oil change in a timely manner, allowing you to spend time doing the things you enjoy. No appointment necessary.
